About AIXelerate Challenge

Student-Led, Mission-Driven, Open to All.

AIXelerate Challenge helps middle and high school students build confidence, technical judgment, and civic imagination through practical project-based learning.

Our Mission

Make advanced technology education reachable.

AIXelerate Challenge is dedicated to breaking down barriers to advanced computer science and technology education. We equip middle and high school students from varied backgrounds with resources, mentorship, and opportunities to build projects that address meaningful problems.

We believe the strongest learning happens when students work on real constraints: users, deadlines, ethics, communication, and teamwork. Our programs are free so that cost does not determine who gets to participate.

Fiscal Sponsorship

Hack Club Bank

AIXelerate Challenge is a registered project under the fiscal sponsorship of Hack Club Bank, a global nonprofit program supporting student technical initiatives.

Through Hack Club Bank, AIXelerate Challenge operates under Hack Club's 501(c)(3) tax-exempt status, allowing us to receive donations and manage program funds transparently.

On the Road

Learning Alongside the AI Community.

Earlier this year, members of our core team attended an AI conference to connect with builders, researchers, and other student programs working in the space. We came back with a clearer sense of what makes student AI projects genuinely useful — and a few new ideas for how we run the Build Sprint.

Conversations there shaped how we think about mentorship, responsible design, and giving students real feedback instead of just a grade. It's part of why our judging rubric weighs impact and feasibility as heavily as raw technical execution.
