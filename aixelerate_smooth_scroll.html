About the Challenge

Empowering the Next Generation of AI Builders.

The AIXelerate Challenge is a student-led AI competition for high schoolers ages 14–18. Participants build passion-driven projects that tackle real-world problems using artificial intelligence.

The competition is beginner-friendly, completely free, and guides students step-by-step from raw idea to live presentation.

Judging Criteria

How You're Scored

100 core points with bonus opportunities for outstanding work.

Problem & Impact

25 pts

Is the problem real and meaningful? Who benefits and does it actually matter?

AI Understanding & Use

25 pts

Is AI genuinely needed? Is it clearly explained without relying on buzzwords?

Execution / Prototype

25 pts

Did you build something? Does it work, even at a basic level? Effort counts.

Creativity & Feasibility

25 pts

Is the idea original? Could it realistically work and scale in the real world?

⭐ Bonus Points

User feedback included+5 pts
Public voting participation+5 pts

FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need coding experience to participate? ▼
No coding experience is required. The AIXelerate Challenge is designed for beginners. You'll learn as you go, and our resources guide you through understanding and applying AI concepts without needing to write complex code.
Can I enter as a solo student? ▼
Absolutely! You can enter as an individual or as part of a team. There are no restrictions on team size for the first two stages.
Is there a registration fee? ▼
The AIXelerate Challenge is 100% free to enter. We believe financial barriers should never stop a great idea from getting into the world.
What kind of AI projects qualify? ▼
Any project that genuinely uses AI to solve a real-world problem — mental health, education, environment, accessibility, and more. AI must be a meaningful part of the solution, not just a buzzword.
How does the mentor program work? ▼
Mentors are matched with student teams in Stage 2. They provide guidance on AI implementation, pitch preparation, and ethical considerations. Sessions are virtual and flexible around schedules.
When is the competition? ▼
The 2025 season is now open for applications. Sign up to receive the full timeline and important dates directly to your inbox.
