Year-Round

Initiatives and Workshops.

Beyond the flagship event, AIXelerate Challenge runs learning circles, panels, and project support sessions throughout the year.

Fall

Technical Workshop Series

Hands-on virtual workshops that help students plan projects, build simple prototypes, organize demos, and explain design choices.

Winter

Responsible Design Panels

Structured discussions on privacy, accessibility, safety, bias prevention, and building projects that can be explained clearly.

Spring

Prototype Incubation

A mentorship track for selected projects to refine their scope, improve documentation, and prepare for public showcase.

Monthly

Community Office Hours

Open support sessions for students seeking help with team formation, project planning, presentations, and next steps.

Seasonal

Mentor Briefings

Training sessions for mentors and judges to align around feedback quality, review criteria, and student support.

Showcase

Project Demo Review

Post-event review opportunities for teams that want feedback before sharing their project with a broader audience.
