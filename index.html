Our Vision

Purposeful Projects, Practical Skills.

Students identify real problems, build thoughtful prototypes, and explain their work clearly. The challenge rewards usefulness, responsible design, teamwork, and communication.

Define the Problem

Choose a specific user need, community issue, or operational challenge and describe why it matters.

Build with Mentorship

Work with peers and mentors to shape the prototype, evaluate feasibility, and prepare a clear presentation.

Present Responsibly

Explain tradeoffs, risks, privacy considerations, accessibility, and how the project could be used in practice.

Competition Tracks

Choose a Track.

Each track is broad enough for original ideas and specific enough to keep projects grounded.

Track 01

Education

Empowering learners and teachers through clearer instruction, access, feedback, and classroom support.

Track 02

Sports

Enhancing performance, athletics, training, strategy, team coordination, and safe participation.

Track 03

Mental Health

Supporting mindfulness, wellness, accessibility, resource navigation, and healthy daily routines.

Track 04

Community Impact

Solving local neighborhood and environmental problems with practical, service-oriented tools.

Track 05

Animal Welfare

Improving shelter operations, adoption matching, habitat awareness, veterinary access, and care coordination.

Track 06

Public Safety

Helping communities prepare, communicate, and respond to everyday hazards and emergency situations.
