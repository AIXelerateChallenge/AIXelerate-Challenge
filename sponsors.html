Sponsorship Opportunities

Ways to Support the Challenge.

Sponsorship isn't limited to cash — in-kind support is just as valuable.

Prize Funding

Cash Prizes

Fund or add to our cash prize pool for top-performing teams, or sponsor a themed award category.

Venue Support

Space & Facilities

Help cover or provide venue space, catering, or day-of facilities for the in-person Build Sprint.

Tools & Credits

Software & APIs

Provide API credits, software licenses, or developer tools students can use to build their projects — like our current Claude credits prize.

Mentorship

People Power

Send engineers, designers, or researchers to mentor teams during the build window.

Judging

Evaluation

Contribute judges to help evaluate final projects at the expo and score against our rubric.

Operational Support

Event Funding

General funding to support event operations, supplies, participant swag, or transportation assistance.

Sponsor Recognition

What Sponsors Receive.

Logo Placement

Your logo featured on our website, event signage, and printed or digital materials.

Ceremony Recognition

A shout-out during opening remarks and the awards & closing ceremony.

Social Media

Acknowledgement across our Instagram and other social channels before and after the event.

Direct Visibility

Face time with students, educators, mentors, and judges attending the event in person.
